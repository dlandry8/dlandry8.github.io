Welcome to Landarian Labs!

Landarian Labs is a place dedicated to lifelong learning and discovery, especially computer technology. For now, it is a place that showcases the work and life of Landryland Labs' founder David Landry. But, we here at Landryland Labs have a passion for collaborating with others with a passion for technology and for using it to solve difficult real-world problems. So, be sure to visit regularly and look for new opportunities to collaborate, learn, and teach.
